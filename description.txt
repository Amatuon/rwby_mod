Dust a RWBY Mod [WIP]

Explore Terraria with weapons from team RWBY!


Adds the signiture weapon for each of the members of team RWBY. Right click a weapon to change its mode.
Weapons from team RWBY partially complete. Weapons function as intended but switching between weapon modes is not fully supported yet.
